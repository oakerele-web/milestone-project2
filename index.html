Random Number Guess Game!

You are welcome to play an exciting number guess game!
It's both challenging and relaxing at the sametime.

Instructions

The game RANDOMLY picks a numberbetween 0 and 99 that is known only to the Console.
The player then has a chance of making 10 guesses to unveil the mystery number. Each time a guess is made, a message comes up to announce one of the following:
a) That's lower! Guess: _ Remaining: _ (so player will know he/she must guess higher figure to get the mystery number)
b) That's higher! Guess: _ Remaining: _ (similar to above, it to guide the player on what to guess next)
c) No more guesses remaining! The number was _
d) Yes, its _! It took you _ guess(es)
e) Refresh to play it again.
Now you can start playing the game and I assure you that you will find it interesting. Enjoy!
